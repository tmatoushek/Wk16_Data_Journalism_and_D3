Correlations of Lifestyle Health Risks, Age, and Income

A positive correlation exists between poverty rates and; lack of healthcare, obesity and smoking. The strongest correlation exists between poverty rates and lack of healthcare, with obesity and smoking having weaker correlations.

A negative correlation exists between age and lack of healthcare. A slightly positive correlation exists between age and smoking. There does not seem to be a positive or negative correlation between age and obesity, however, in general obesity rates are hovering around 30% for all age / state data.

A negative correlation exists between household income and; lack of healthcare, obesity and smoking.
